CIVIC ENGAGEMENT

Over the past few years, I have participated in numerous activities related to ecology and gender-based and sexual violence.Below is a non-exhaustive chronological list of the activities I have attended.

My civic engagement

Fresques écologiques

Over the past few years, I have attended many ecological frescoes. For example, I attended the climate frescoes twice and then once each for the biodiversity, digital and planetary boundaries frescoes. In 2024, I also attended the two-tonne workshop that took place at the INP preparatory school.

King Kong trial

I also attended two performances of an interactive play called "The Trial of King Kong". This play deals with gender-based and sexual violence in the student community. Hosted by a criminal court judge, this play was very informative.

"Futur proche" festival

I participated in the one-day "Futur proches" festival for two consecutive years. During these days, I attended a conference on the ecological impact of AI, a presentation on the Matilda effect, a presentation on how to eat responsibly, and a videoconference with an economist author on the idea of economic degrowth.
